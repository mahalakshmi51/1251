Sasi Institute of Technology and Engineering was established in the year 2002, under the aegis of the Sasi Group of Institutions. The campus of the Sasi Institute of Technology and Engineering, Tadepalligudem is spread across 28 acres of area. The institute is situated in the West Godavari District of Tadepalligudem, Andhra Pradesh, Hyderabad. Sasi Institute of Technology and Engineering, Tadepalligudem is affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Kakinada. The institute has been accredited by NAAC - National Assessment and Accreditation Council and along with NBA - National Board of Accreditation
